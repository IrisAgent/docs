## Sign up on IrisAgent Dashboard

- Go to the [IrisAgent Dashboard](https://web.irisagent.com/) and sign in with either GSuite, Microsoft, or Okta SSO.
  <br/>
  <img 
    style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
    src="/images/web-login.png" 
    alt="IrisAgent app listing on Zendesk"

  />
<br/>
- Click on **Connect with Zendesk**. Give the OAuth permissions and click on Allow. This will automatically connect with both Zendesk tickets and Zendesk Guide/Knowledge.
<br>
  <img 
    style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
    src="/images/zd-connect.png" 
    alt="IrisAgent app listing on Zendesk"

  />
<br/>
- This step is optional. If you want to connect with more platforms, such as Jira, Confluence, Salesforce, etc., navigate to **Manage Integrations** on the bottom left and connect with the relevant platforms.

## **Install the IrisAgent app into your Zendesk account**

- Navigate to [IrisAgent's listing](https://www.zendesk.com/marketplace/apps/support/244660/irisagent/) on the Zendesk Marketplace.
- Click on **Install** and follow the next steps.
