## **Introduction**

IrisAgent AI can answer customer queries right within your Intercom chatbot seamlessly. This guide will help you connect IrisAgent with your Intercom account.

## **Setup**

1. Navigate to the [Chat setup](https://web.irisagent.com/manage-chatbot/chat) page in your IrisAgent dashboard.
2. Click the **Connect** button on the Intercom integration card.
3. Follow the authorization prompts to grant IrisAgent access to your Intercom account.

    <img
      style={{ borderRadius:"0.5rem" }}
      src="/images/intercom_config_page.png"
      alt="IrisAgent Intercom configuration page"
    />

Once connected, IrisAgent will automatically handle incoming conversations and respond to customer queries in your Intercom chatbot.

## **Configuration**

After connecting, click the **gear icon** (⚙) on the Intercom integration card to open the configuration dialog.

<img
  style={{ borderRadius:"0.5rem" }}
  src="/images/intercom-chat-filter.png"
  alt="Intercom Chat Configuration dialog"
/>

### Enable Chat Automation

Toggle this on to activate the AI assistant on your Intercom chatbot. When disabled, IrisAgent will not respond to incoming conversations.

### Chatbot User ID

Set the User ID for the chatbot. This determines which Intercom user the AI assistant responds as, allowing you to select a dedicated technical user for bot replies.

### Bot Behavior

Control when and how the AI assistant responds to messages.

- **Hand-off Trigger** — Choose the condition that causes the AI assistant to stop responding automatically, allowing your human agents to take over. For example, *"When an agent sends a message"*.

- **Escalation Routing** — When enabled, conversations are automatically routed to a specific teammate or team when customers request human support.

### Chat Filter

When enabled, allows you to filter which conversations the AI assistant responds to.

Click **Save** to apply your changes.
