IrisAgent helps agents find AI answers to tickets at their fingertips using the Copilot app right within your ticketing system. The Copilot sidebar surfaces AI-suggested resolutions, case summaries, sentiment, similar cases, linked Jira issues, and more — directly inside the ticket view. For a full walkthrough of the sidebar features, see the [Agent Assist / Copilot App](/agent-copilot) guide.
