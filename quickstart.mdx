Get your first AI Agent running in minutes with the IrisAgent onboarding wizard. Sign up at [web.irisagent.com](https://web.irisagent.com) and the wizard launches automatically on your first login.

## Get started in three steps

### Step 1: Connect your first data source

The onboarding wizard walks you through two paths depending on what you want to build:

- **Ticketing system** (Zendesk, Freshdesk, HubSpot, Jira, Zoho, or Outlook): connect via OAuth and IrisAgent starts importing your tickets, macros, and knowledge base articles. Best for AI suggested resolutions, agent copilot, and AutoQA.
- **Website link**: paste your URL and IrisAgent builds an AI agent trained on your public site and help docs in minutes. Best for chat deflection on your marketing site or help center.

Not ready to self-serve? Select **Speak to a human** in the wizard to book a 20-minute demo instead.

### Step 2: Deploy IrisAgent AI

The wizard's final step gives you the fastest paths to deploy:

- **Embed the chat widget** on your website (snippet is generated for you)
- **Install the agent copilot** sidebar inside your ticketing system
- **Invite teammates** to the workspace

For all deployment options see the [Deploying IrisAgent](/deploying-irisagent/Chatbot-on-Website) guides.

### Step 3: Connect additional data sources

After the wizard, continue growing your agent's knowledge from the dashboard:

- Knowledge bases (Confluence, Notion, Zendesk Guide)
- File uploads (PDF, DOCX, plain text)
- Q&A pairs and custom APIs

See [Connecting Data Sources](/data-sources/Zendesk) for the full list.
