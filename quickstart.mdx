## Get started in three steps

Get your first AI Agent running.

### Step 1: Connect your ticketing system

Head to **Ticketing Systems** section below to connect your ticketing system and give IrisAgent access to your tickets and knowledge to start training.


### Step 2: Deploy IrisAgent AI

Choose from one or more options to deploy AI to better serve your customers:

- Chatbot on your website
- AI bot for your Slack or Teams
- AI Agents for email
- AI Agents for voice
- Agent copilot for your team

### Step 3: Connect with additional data sources

Connect IrisAgent with your Knowledge Bases, Bug trackers, CRM, and more to give your AI Agents your full enterprise context.
