## Sign up on IrisAgent Dashboard

- Go to the [IrisAgent Dashboard](https://web.irisagent.com/) and sign in with either GSuite, Microsoft, or Okta SSO.

  <br />

  <img
    style={{ borderRadius:"0.5rem" }}
    src="/images/web-login.png"
    alt="IrisAgent web app login"
  />

<br />

- Click on **Connect with Outlook**. You will be asked to provide the following details:
  - **Email Inbox**: The email address of the mailbox used for ticket ingestion.
  - **Tenant ID**: Your Azure account identifier. You can find your Tenant ID by following [Microsoft's guide](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/sharepoint/find-your-office-365-tenant-id).

  <br />

  <img
    style={{ borderRadius:"0.5rem" }}
    src="/images/zd-connect.png"
    alt="IrisAgent integration options including Outlook"
  />

<br />

- After submitting the form, you will be redirected to Microsoft OAuth to authorize IrisAgent. The following permissions will be requested: `Mail.Read`, `Mail.Send`, and `offline_access`.

<Note>
We recommend using a dedicated service account for the OAuth connection rather than a personal Microsoft account. This ensures uninterrupted access if the individual account owner leaves the organization.
</Note>

<br />

- This step is optional. If you want to connect with more platforms, such as Jira, Confluence, Salesforce, etc., navigate to **Manage Integrations** on the bottom left and connect with the relevant platforms.
